Brother and sister

Hein started in the 1970s as a pioneer with a small-scale bakery for organic biscuits. This is what his children (Annemiek and Frans), the love for organic biscuits literally got into their hearts. With the greatest respect for family traditions, they professionalised the operation of the bakery. Despite the sharp increase in production volumes, Eco Biscuits still swears by traditional craftsmanship with home-made recipes.

Frans

Frans continuously develops and works on the development of new recipes to gradually expand the range of biscuits. In addition to the family tradition of living organic and healthy, he obtained a baker's diploma from the PIVA in Antwerp. This gives him both the necessary craftsmanship and the theoretical knowledge to develop healthy, delicious organic biscuits.

Annemiek

Annemiek weighs and weighs and focuses mainly on the business side of Eco Biscuits. Of course she tastes the test biscuits. Before a new cookie comes on the market, there is a lot to consider. Both Frans and Annemiek can still be found daily at the cookie ovens and the packaging lines.
