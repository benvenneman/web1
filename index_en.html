Eco Biscuits

Biscuits biologics

Eco Biscuits prepares organic and vegan biscuits in-house. Our delicious and healthy biscuits are available in more than 700 organic shops in Belgium and the Netherlands. With the convenient one-piece packaging, you'll have a healthy snack at your fingertips anywhere, anytime. Or would you prefer to buy by the box? Don't forget to check our assortment

Own recipes

Our organic biscuits are the result of more than 40 years of passion for everything that is tasty, healthy and organic.
Each organic biscuit is developed in-house with a great love for the bakery business. In our biscuits, you will not find any artificial additives or beet and cane sugar. We only use organic raw materials.

Pure indulgence for body and mind

Pure indulgence for body and mind, those are the organic biscuits from Eco Biscuits. Always and everywhere! A fragrant cup of coffee tastes so much better with one of our tasty organic cookies. You can easily overcome an energy dip in the office with one of our Bio Snacks in one piece packaging. An organic biscuit from Eco Biscuits as a snack at school is always a winner.

Between ideology and commerce

Eco Biscuits aims for a balance between modernisation, commercialisation and the preservation of valuable natural food traditions.
That is why Eco Biscuits invests in modern production methods and packaging techniques. At the same time, Eco Biscuits focuses on a number of qualities valued by consumers.
